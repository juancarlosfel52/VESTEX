What Is Paper Trading?

Paper trading is how professional traders train before going live. You receive $1,000,000 in simulated funds and trade against real market prices — the same live data VESTEX uses for every prediction and signal.

Every buy, sell, gain, and loss is tracked exactly as it would be in a real brokerage account. The only difference: none of it uses real money.

⚡
How To Use It
1
Study the signals. Check Master Intelligence, predictions, and Brain Vault patterns before making a move.
2
Place a trade. Enter a symbol and share count — your simulated account executes at the live market price.
3
Track your P&L. Watch your positions move in real time. Learn what works and what doesn't — at no cost.
4
Review your history. Every trade is logged. Study your decisions the same way professionals review trade journals.
◆ The Self-Learning Loop

Every trade you make here creates a feedback loop. When you buy based on a VESTEX signal and the trade wins — you learn to trust that signal. When it loses — you learn when not to follow it.

This is exactly how professional traders develop intuition. Over weeks of paper trading, patterns in your own decisions emerge: which signals you over-trust, which stocks you over-trade, when you exit too early. No textbook teaches this — only repetition with real data does.

The goal is simple: when you're ready to trade with real money, you already know how you behave under pressure.
