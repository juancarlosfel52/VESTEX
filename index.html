How We Score Ourselves
Complete transparency on our method
✓
CORRECT — Direction was right
We said "Going Up" and the price closed higher after 7 days. Or we said "Going Down" and it fell. Flat is correct if the price moved less than 0.5% either way.
✗
WRONG — Direction was off
We said "Going Up" but the price fell more than 0.5%. Every wrong call makes us improve the model for next week.
◷
PENDING — Still waiting
The 7-day window hasn't passed yet. Come back at the end of the week to see if we were right.
